The first online typography

PrintBox.io connects an unlimited number of printing equipment into a single network. Clients access the network through a common interface printbox.io , allowing them to print any order in 3 clicks and instantly receive it within walking distance. One of the foundations of the project is the concept of sharing economy, allowing anyone with a printer to join the network.

Service work

Remote control of the printer and scanner, processing payments and sending checks to customers, document processing, dynamic print pricing depending on filling, sending a print order or scan to any printbox network equipment

From idea to product

  • Q4 2017

    Calculation and creation of a business model for the future concept of a print service that unites an unlimited number of print points and printing equipment into a single network, providing the user with a single interface for interacting with it.

  • DECEMBER 2017

    Proof of concept. Created the first simple printing point. We started our service within a limited number of users. First incomes and feedback from our users. The launch showed the relevance of the concept and the relevance of the business model in the selected user segment. The project was awarded the Skolkovo Digital Peaks Prize.

  • MARCH 2018

    The project entered the accelerator “Fiztech.Start”

  • APRIL 2018

    Created MVP vending machine made the necessary documentation and drawings

  • MAY 2018

    10 vending machines (the first small-scale lot) were delivered to Bauman State Technical University (BMSTU) (1 by request of Mail.ru Group) and Moscow Institute of Physics and Technologies (MIPT) (9 vending machines). Launched an affiliate loyalty program with Mail.Ru training projects.

  • AUGUST 2018

    Officially registered company LLC “PBX Solutions”. Created a package of documents for the development of the project in the B2B market and franchise sales.

  • OCTOBER 2018

    The PrintBox vending machine has been modified to the market version, the company and product have been advertised on the market for a limited number of customers.

  • DECEMBER 2018

    3 contracts were signed with the network of grocery stores VkusVill for sublease for the PrintBox-standard automatic printing machine for a period of 6 months.

  • FEBRUARY 2018

    Launched a campaign of active B2B sales and distribution of the PrintBox franchise.
